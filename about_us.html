About EcoHub

EcoHub is a purpose-driven platform integrating environmental responsibility, infrastructure, and innovation to deliver practical, sustainable solutions.

The work is grounded in lived experience — shaped by hands-on engagement across water systems, media, community initiatives, and development-focused communication.

Founded by Evans Gitau, EcoHub exists to bridge vision with execution, ensuring sustainability is not abstract, but measurable, scalable, and impactful.

  • • Environmental stewardship & climate-conscious solutions
  • • Infrastructure support & system-based thinking
  • • Communication, media & community empowerment
Our Mission

To build sustainable systems where environmental responsibility and infrastructure work together to create lasting value for communities.

Our Vision

To become a trusted hub for sustainable development, empowering people, systems, and environments to thrive.
