Kenya’s Water Revolution — Strategic Sustainability for Growth

Strategic Vision

During my time with Nairobi City Water and Sewerage Company, I witnessed first-hand the challenges and opportunities in sustainable water management. Inspired by this, I now advocate for scalable, resilient, and inclusive strategies that align with Kenya’s Vision 2030 and the Water Sector Investment Plan.

  • Resilience through infrastructure
  • Youth and community empowerment
  • Integrated environmental planning

Target Collaboration: Athi Water Works, NCWSC, Ministry of Water, NIA

Transforming Water for a Resilient Kenya

The Ministry of Water and Sanitation (MWS) is currently undertaking an ambitious agenda for the Kenya water sector through a long-term, transformational program aimed at building water security and climate resilience for economic growth and social well-being.

This is in recognition of water as an enabler for the achievement of the Government’s Big Four Agenda and improved livelihoods for all.
