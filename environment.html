What We Do

From clean streets to vibrant parks, we are reimagining urban living sustainably.

Street & Drainage Cleaning

We keep streets and drainage systems clean, preventing floods and ensuring healthy surroundings.

Recycling & Waste Management

We collect waste, recycle, and transform materials into useful eco-friendly products.

Tree Planting & Parks

We plant trees and maintain private & public parks to enhance biodiversity and fresh air.

Bins & Clean City Initiative

Strategically placing and maintaining bins to reduce littering in city centers.

Street Security

Partnering with local authorities to offer security and make communities safer.

Gated Communities

Transforming ungated neighborhoods into safe, organized, and gated communities.
